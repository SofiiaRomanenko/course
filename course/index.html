OUR SERVICES

THERAPEUTIC RECEPTION

Therapeutic reception is a very important part of the work of the clinic’s doctors. Almost every patient, having come to the clinic, first of all gets to the general practitioner - this is a specialist who carefully communicates with the owner, finds out complaints and symptoms of the disease, conducts a full examination of the animal in order to identify the cause of the disease.

SURGERY

Surgical unit: there is often a struggle for the life of the patient. The result depends on the coordinated work of a whole team of specialists - anesthetists, surgeons, assistants. The work of modern surgery is high-tech: apparatuses for monitoring the patient's condition in anesthesia, artificial lung ventilation apparatus, oxygen concentrator, apparatus for biological tissue welding

STATIONARY

The animal hospital, where they are under the supervision of a doctor 24 hours a day, receive the necessary care, diagnostic and treatment procedures. Here the animals are in individual boxes with a transparent door, ventilation and "warm floor". The hospital has all the necessary care items - bowls, leashes, bedding, toilets and fillers.

ONCOLOGY TREATMENT

Oncology or tumors in dogs and cats are a very common problem. In this area, as in no other, the principle of early diagnosis is relevant. Indeed, the identification and treatment of a tumor at an early stage can save a pet’s life, significantly reduce the course and range of therapeutic drugs, and reduce the amount of surgical intervention.
